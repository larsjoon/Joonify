Honest Privacy.

Last updated: December 2025

1. What we collect

We believe in data minimization. When you visit joonify.dev, we collect:

  • Aggregate Stats: We count page views and visitor countries using Cloudflare Analytics. This helps us understand our global reach.
  • The 3D Map: To light up the globe, we process your rough location (Country level). We do not store your IP address or pinpoint your location.

2. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. We use Local Storage solely to remember if you have dismissed this privacy banner.

3. Contact Form

If you email us, your name and email are sent securely via our API and stored only for the purpose of replying to you. We do not sell your data.
