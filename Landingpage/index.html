Joonify

Crafting small language models with a big impact

Joonify is a pioneering student startup based in Noord Holland, Netherlands, focused on developing SLMs (Small Language Models) tailored for specific, efficient applications.

We combine cutting-edge AI technology with a personal, agile approach to empower educators, researchers, and businesses with customized, accessible models that don't compromise on performance.

The name "Joonify" draws from the Persian word "Joon" (جون), meaning "soul" or "dear one" — a reflection of our mission to infuse our models with purpose and care.
